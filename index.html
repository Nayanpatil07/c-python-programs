Projects

Smart Construction Helmet & Inflatable Vest with SOS System

IoT ESP32 Embedded C Industrial Safety

The Problem: Falls from heights cause severe injuries in construction. Standard gear leaves vital organs exposed.

The Solution: A wearable IoT safety system that acts as a digital reflex.

  • Real-Time Fall Detection: Uses an MPU6050 sensor to calculate free-fall parameters.
  • Active Protection: ESP32 logic automatically triggers a relay to inflate a safety vest prior to impact.
  • Automated SOS: Transmits real-time distress signals via Wi-Fi to site supervisors while activating local auditory alarms.
